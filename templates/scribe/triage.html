Tips
  • MMS works best on 16 kHz mono WAV/MP3, < 30 s per chunk.
  • First run downloads ~4 GB of weights to the HuggingFace cache.
  • CPU is fine for batch / post-encounter; CUDA needed for live streaming.
  • Set SCRIBE_ENABLE_TRIAGE=True in .env to share with non-staff.
